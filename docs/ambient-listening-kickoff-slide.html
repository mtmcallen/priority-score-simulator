Ambient Listening Implementation Plan

Rounders speak naturally at the bedside; AI drafts script responses from the conversation. Human-in-the-loop — nothing auto-submits until the rounder reviews and submits.

What We're Implementing

Speak naturally at the bedside. AI handles the documentation.

  • Consent-first workflow — patient consent captured before any recording begins
  • Human-in-the-loop — AI drafts responses; rounders review, edit, and submit
  • Mobile + web — iOS v6.13.0+ / Android v2.3.0+; microphone permissions required
  • North star metric — Capture Rate ≥85% (successful ambient sessions vs. eligible encounters)

Implementation Phases & Timeline

Week 1 · Initiate

Kickoff & Alignment

  • Confirm scope, scripts, units, and timeline
  • Identify operational stakeholders and super-user champions
  • Align on success metrics — Capture Rate ≥85% and baseline doc time

Weeks 2–3 · Design

Script & Consent Readiness

  • Review conversational phrasing on in-scope scripts
  • Nursing leaders approve consent language and workflow
  • Confirm device readiness (iOS 6.13+ / Android 2.3+, mic permissions)

Weeks 4–5 · Configure

Evolve & Reporting

  • 3-click enablement: CP setting → script toggle → role permission
  • Set up dashboard access and ambient listening data views
  • Complete pre-go-live checklist (consent, training plan, baseline metrics)

Week 6 · Testing

UAT on Devices

  • Test consent Given / Declined paths on live devices
  • Validate processing (~20–30 sec) and Needs Review queue
  • Resolve config gaps before end-user training

Week 7 · Training

Enable Rounders

  • Train on notetaking assistant messaging and consent workflow
  • Emphasize review before submit — rounder owns final documentation
  • Activate super-users for peer coaching; share hype flyer & training videos

Week 8+ · Launch

Go-Live & Optimize

  • Rounders use ambient listening in daily workflow
  • Biweekly feedback on adoption, consent rates, and processing failures
  • Monitor Capture Rate and script completion vs. baseline

From this meeting

Customer commitments

  • Nursing leaders approve consent language and script phrasing
  • Rounders test on live shifts and join biweekly feedback sessions
  • EC / PC drive adoption; identify a super-user for peer coaching
