Ambient Listening

Speak naturally at the bedside. AI handles the documentation.

Ambient Listening listens to the patient conversation, drafts script answers, and puts them in Needs Review — rounders always review and submit. Nothing auto-submits.

What it is

Your notetaking assistant in CipherRounds

  • Built into rounding — no new app; works on mobile and web
  • Consent first — recording starts only after patient consent
  • Human-in-the-loop — AI suggests answers; rounders edit and submit

How to turn it on

Three clicks in Evolve

  1. 1
    Enable Ambient Listening Turn on at the care provider level in Evolve
  2. 2
    Toggle the script Enable AI Ambient Listening on the rounding script
  3. 3
    Grant permission Add the Ambient Listening role permission for rounders

That's it. Rounders need iOS v6.13.0+ or Android v2.3.0+ with microphone access — then they're ready to go.
