What is Staff SSR?

Web-based self-service surveys for staff within CipherRounds. Staff authenticate with First Name, Last Name, Email, and Employee ID, select an eligible program, and submit feedback on any device.

  • Check-ins, satisfaction surveys, new hire rounds, exit interviews — and more
  • Structured questions recommended for service recovery (Y/N, multiple choice)
  • Multiple submissions allowed; availability can be 24/7 or limited to date ranges
  • Complements traditional leader-led staff rounding — it doesn't replace your rounding culture
