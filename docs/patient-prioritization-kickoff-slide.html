Patient Prioritization Implementation Plan

Advisory priority scores (0–100) on the CipherRounds worklist — configurable factors, transparent reasoning, and optional AI for HCAHPS risk. Scores are advisory and do not replace clinical judgment.

What We're Implementing

Most tools show a score. We show the why.

  • Priority tags on every patient card — sort and filter the worklist at shift start
  • Factor breakdown — rounders see what drove each score before entering the room
  • Configurable logic — rule-based factors per unit; optional Likelihood to be Dissatisfied AI factor
  • No new app — enabled in Evolve; visible on CipherRounds mobile and web

Implementation Phases & Timeline

Week 1 · Initiate

Kickoff & Alignment

  • Confirm scope, units, and timeline
  • Identify operational stakeholders and rounder champions
  • Review success metrics and feedback cadence

Weeks 2–3 · Design

Factor Discovery

  • Decide contributing factors (LOS, flags, last round, issues, etc.)
  • Agree on factor weights with nursing leadership
  • Model configs in the Priority Score Simulator

Weeks 4–5 · Configure

Evolve Build

  • Enable feature flag + provider role permissions
  • Build & activate factor program(s)
  • Set activation criteria for unit-specific programs

Week 6 · Testing

Validate

  • Verify scores on mobile and web worklists
  • Confirm sort/filter and factor detail views
  • Resolve config gaps before end-user training

Week 7 · Training

Enable Rounders

  • Train on reading tags, sorting, and factor breakdown
  • Socialize that scores are advisory — clinical judgment always applies
  • Distribute setup one-pager and FAQ resources

Week 8+ · Launch

Go-Live & Optimize

  • Rounders use priority scores in daily workflow
  • Weekly → biweekly feedback sessions with stakeholders
  • Tune factor weights based on frontline input

From this meeting

Customer commitments

  • Nursing leaders join factor/weight design sessions (~2 hrs kickoff block)
  • Rounders test during live shifts and join feedback sessions
  • EC / PC drive awareness and remove adoption blockers
