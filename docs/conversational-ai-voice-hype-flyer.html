What is Conversational AI for Voice and SMS?

Conversational AI enhances existing CipherOutreach programs by letting patients respond in their own words while staying within your established script structure. AI interprets free-text or spoken answers, maps them to structured responses, and records verbatim patient language for callback teams.

  • SMS — enable AI Enhanced on applicable questions; remove “Reply 1, 2, etc.” prompts
  • Voice — natural-language responses on live phone outreach (same AI mapping, voice transcriptions in interaction history)
  • Skip — remove questions from future outreach actions when already answered favorably
  • Reflect — personalize transitions between questions based on prior answers (question groups required)

Example: Skip & Reflect in action

Day 1: patient says “I’m tired and sore.” Day 5: the program reflects back — “Roger, you didn’t feel well yesterday. How do you feel today?” — and skips the medication question because it was already answered favorably.
